Dragonmaster

Dragonmaster: The Dragonai tribe have lived in the Himalayas for centuries, and rarely leave their mountains. As an exception, Dragonai who have bonded well with a particular dragon go in search of great legends to fight, only to become legends themselves, the Dragonmasters.
They usually bond with one of five particular dragons:
The Chinese Fireball, Welsh Green, Hungarian Horntail, Swedish Short-Snout, or the Japanese Wind-Waker.

Name of Special: Searing Spear
Triggered: When health is under 20
Effect: Dragonmaster's rage is raised by 5 and deals opponent 15 damage.
