Valkyrie

Valkyrie: Strong and wild, their victory cries echo throughout the skies and bring hope into the hearts of the innocent and fear into the hearts of the unholy. All Valkyrie's gather at Yggdrasil for leisure, and Asgard for times of war. Their soft eyes and smiles hide ferocity and passion. They treat their winged horses as family and talk to them as if they were human. Merciless and savage, yet beautiful.

Triggered: When health is under 30
Effect: Valkyrie's rage is raised by 10 and opponent is dealt 5 damage
